About


Find a QT's inception was with a class project in 2020. The team noticed a gap between tutoring services and students at UVA. After further inspection, we realized that most UVA students in our sample didn't use the tutoring services offered by the university. Because of this, we decided to invest our time into this project and make it our project for the semester.


Our primary goal is to connect students with tutors. The student simply creates an account, snaps a picture of the question and chooses its topic and our tutors get notified. After a tutor accepts the session, the student and tutor meet according to the details of the session.


With this service, the student only needs to pay for the amount of time required and not a cent more!
